Main Auditorium

"The encryption we use today will be obsolete by breakfast."

Alex Chen has spent the last decade dismantling the boundaries between classical and quantum computing. As the lead architect for the Chimera-Q project, he spearheaded the development of the world's first stable 1000-qubit processor functioning at room temperature.

KEYNOTE SESSION

Entanglement & The Death of Privacy

In this talk, Alex will demonstrate a live decryption attack using a cloud-based quantum simulator, followed by a roadmap for Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC). He will discuss how developers in 2026 must adapt their security protocols to survive the coming "Q-Day".
